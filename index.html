探索“威尔士亲王号” (R09) Explore HMS Prince of Wales (R09)

英国皇家海军的现代巨人 A Modern Titan of the Royal Navy

“威尔士亲王号”是英国皇家海军伊丽莎白女王级的第二艘航空母舰，代表了海军工程和航空技术的巅峰。本页将通过一系列图片，带您深入了解这艘先进战舰的各个组成部分和关键设备。 HMS Prince of Wales is the second of the Royal Navy's Queen Elizabeth-class aircraft carriers, representing a pinnacle of naval engineering and aviation technology. This page will take you deep into this advanced warship through a series of images detailing its components and key equipment.

飞行甲板与航空设施 Flight Deck & Aviation Facilities

标志性滑跃甲板Iconic Ski-Jump Ramp

舰艏的滑跃甲板是STOVL航母的关键特征，可帮助F-35B战机以更大载荷起飞。The ski-jump at the bow is a key feature of STOVL carriers, helping the F-35B take off with a heavier payload.

F-35B闪电II战斗机F-35B Lightning II

作为航母的核心打击力量，第五代F-35B战斗机正在进行起飞前的准备。As the carrier's core strike capability, the 5th generation F-35B fighter prepares for take-off.

舷侧飞机升降机Deck-Edge Aircraft Lifts

两台巨大的舷侧升降机能在一分钟内将两架F-35B从机库运送到甲板。Two massive deck-edge lifts can transport two F-35Bs from the hangar to the flight deck in about 60 seconds.

巨大机库Massive Hangar

机库空间巨大，可容纳和维护多种类型的飞机和直升机。The enormous hangar provides space for maintaining various types of aircraft and helicopters.

舰岛与传感器 Superstructure & Sensors

双舰岛结构Twin Island Superstructure

前部舰岛负责航行，后部舰岛负责航空管制，这是女王级航母的独特设计。The forward island is for navigation, the aft for flight control—a unique design of the QEC.

后部舰岛（航空管制）Aft Island (FlyCo)

专用于飞行管制（FLYCO），指挥所有飞行甲板上的航空作业。Dedicated to flight control (FLYCO), directing all aviation operations on the flight deck.

ARTISAN 3D 雷达ARTISAN 3D Radar

后舰岛上的Type 997 Artisan雷达，提供精准的中程对空和对海监视。The Type 997 Artisan radar on the aft island provides precise medium-range air and surface surveillance.

防御武器系统 Defensive Weapon Systems

密集阵近程防御武器系统 (CIWS)Phalanx CIWS

作为最后一道防线，这种20毫米加特林机炮系统能以极高射速拦截来袭导弹。As the last line of defense, this 20mm Gatling gun intercepts incoming missiles at a high rate of fire.

30毫米自动化小口径机炮30mm Automated Small Calibre Guns

用于防御小型快艇等近距离水面威胁。Used for defense against close-in surface threats like fast attack craft.

诱饵发射系统Decoy Launcher System

发射箔条或红外诱饵，用以干扰和偏离敌方雷达或红外制导导弹。Launches chaff or flares to confuse and divert enemy radar or heat-seeking missiles.

动力与工程 Propulsion & Engineering

劳斯莱斯MT30燃气轮机Rolls-Royce MT30 Gas Turbine

航母的“心脏”之一，这两台燃气轮机与柴油发电机共同提供全舰所需电力。One of the carrier's "hearts," providing electrical power alongside diesel generators.

巨型螺旋桨Giant Propellers

两具重达33吨的定距螺旋桨，为这艘65000吨的巨舰提供强劲动力。Two 33-tonne fixed-pitch propellers provide powerful thrust for this 65,000-tonne behemoth.

机舱控制室Machinery Control Room

工程师在这里监控和管理复杂的综合电力推进（IEP）系统。Engineers monitor and manage the complex Integrated Electric Propulsion (IEP) system from here.
